Alice Chung Hsieh, a daughter of Tammy Lin Hsieh and Dr. Raymond Hsieh of Richmond, Va., was married on July 15 to Carlton Eliot Forbes, a son of Giola D. Forbes and Dr. Ronald O. Forbes of Midlothian, Va. Judge Raymond J. Lohier Jr. of the United States Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit, for whom the groom served as a law clerk from 2015 to 2016, officiated at the Hofheimer Building, an event space in Richmond.The couple have dated since they were juniors in high school at the Maggie L. Walker Governor’s School in Richmond.Ms. Hsieh, 30, is a senior associate, focusing on international anticorruption compliance and investigations, at Miller & Chevalier, a law firm in Washington. She graduated from N.Y.U. and received a law degree from Georgetown. Her father is an inorganic chemist in Virginia’s Division of Consolidated Laboratory Services in Richmond. Her mother retired as a director of information technology in the data warehousing division, in Richmond, for Capital One, the credit card company.Mr. Forbes, also 30, recently completed a clerkship with Judge Cornelia T. L. Pillard of the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit. This month, he is to become a law clerk for Justice Stephen G. Breyer of the Supreme Court. The groom graduated from Harvard and received a law degree from Yale. His father, a psychiatrist, retired in May as the medical director of Central State Hospital, a psychiatric facility in Petersburg, Va.
